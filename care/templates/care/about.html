When you need a fresh perspective....


This app was inspired by my own experience with a dementia sufferer. So many websites are eager to tell you what not to say and truthfully, there are many times when a list of what not to do simply won't help.

Caregiver Response is designed to change the way caregivers are able to communicate with their patients or loved ones in a more thoughtful manner. While compassion is always important, knowing phrases that may help to ease distress are vital for communication.

The words we speak can be powerful, so why not use words that have the potential to make the greatest difference in the lives of patients to help ourselves? This app allows the user to look at a situation and receive a solution designed to help shift the moment to one that is more peaceful for both the patient and the caregiver.

Caregiver Response is designed to create a higher quality experience and give the user a better way to communicate, clearly and easily. You will be able to create a user profile that allows you to add resolutions that are helpful for the individual situation & share with family members or colleagues. Our objective is to help guide you while learning from your experiences to create improved solutions for all.
